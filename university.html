About This Program

The B.Tech CSE course at ITM Skills University is designed to blend technical fundamentals with real-world experience. Students work on projects from day one and are trained to build portfolios that stand out in the global tech industry.

Placements & Industry Support

ISU partners with 700+ companies to support placements and internship opportunities. Students get help building portfolios, preparing for interviews, and showcasing projects on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn.
